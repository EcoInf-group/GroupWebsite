B-cubed

B-Cubed leverages the concept of data cubes to standardise access to biodiversity data using the Essential Biodiversity Variables framework. These cubes, provided by GBIF, are the basis for models and indicators of past, current and future biodiversity. The models will be accessible to users in real-time and on-demand in a cloud computing environment, delivering information on biodiversity status and change. To ensure its results can be reused, the project creates exemplar automated workflows for modelling using biodiversity data cubes and for the calculation of change indicators.
